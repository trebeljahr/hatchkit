# Code of Conduct

Hatchkit contributors are expected to keep issues, discussions, reviews, and pull requests focused, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and specific.
- Assume good intent while still naming real bugs and risks clearly.
- Respect different levels of experience with TypeScript, deployment, infra, and provider tooling.
- Keep security-sensitive details private.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or secrets.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers ask to refocus.
- Dismissive or hostile review behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; lock conversations; or block contributors when needed to keep the project healthy.
